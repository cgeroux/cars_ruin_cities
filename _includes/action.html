What can I do?

Most importantly, if you can, walk, bike, roll, or take public transit to where you need to go. Secondly, get involved in local organizations supporting and or advocating for active and public transit. If you are in the Halifax N.S. Canada area (where these posters originated) I would encourage you to check out the Halifax Cycling Coalition.
If you like these posters, you may want to put up posters in your area. My hope is that putting up posters will make the issue more visible in the real world and make it harder to ignore the negative impacts of cars on our cities. If you would like to put up posters in your local area feel free to download, modify, and or print copies of these poster to put up in your area.
